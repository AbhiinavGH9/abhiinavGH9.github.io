# 🪐 Planet Ripper

> A **monochrome, glassmorphism-inspired 3D destruction simulator** built with Three.js.  
> Rotate, zoom, and rip apart planets with meteors, nukes, and lasers — right inside your browser.  

---

## ╭── ⋅ ⋅ ── ✦ ── ⋅ ⋅ ──╮  
###  FEATURES  
## ╰── ⋅ ⋅ ── ✦ ── ⋅ ⋅ ──╯  

◽ **3D Planets** — Smoothly rotatable, realistic textures.  
◽ **Parallax Background** — Stars and tiny galaxies drifting endlessly.  
◽ **Weapons of Mass Destruction**  
   - ☄️ Meteor  
   - 💥 Nuke  
   - 🔴 Laser  
   *(Toggle on/off with a single click)*  
◽ **Glassmorphism UI** — Home screen overlay, weapon cards, and controls.  
◽ **Reset & Back Controls** — Instantly reset planets or return home.  
◽ **Made with 🧠 by Shukla ji!**  

---

## ╭── ⋅ ⋅ ── ✦ ── ⋅ ⋅ ──╮  
###  HOW TO PLAY  
## ╰── ⋅ ⋅ ── ✦ ── ⋅ ⋅ ──╯  

1. **Start Game** → Launch from the fullscreen home overlay.  
2. **Rotate / Zoom** → Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom.  
3. **Choose a Weapon** → Meteor, Nuke, or Laser.  
4. **Click Planet** → Apply destruction.  
5. **Reset** → Bring your planet back.  
6. **Back Button** → Return home with UI blur.
